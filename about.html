About O-EEF

Here at Omotoyosi Elitefield Educational Foundation Our mission is to provide inclusive, quality education, and position them for better opportunities in life.

Empowering Underprivileged Children through Education

At Omotoyosi Elitefield Educational Foundation, we are dedicated to closing the educational gap for underprivileged children by providing inclusive and high-quality learning opportunities. Our mission is to equip them with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to build brighter futures and create lasting positive change in their communities.

Testimonials to our Achievements

Launched our Saturday Enrichment Program to provide basic literacy and numeracy skills to children in low-income communities.

Initiated a fully-funded, six-year scholarship program for underprivileged children in rural Alabata, Abeokuta, starting in 2023.

Partnered with AIESEC, a non-profit organization, to provide national volunteers for teaching and mentoring.

Collaborated with Alpha Nigeria, increasing our sponsored children from 4 to seven (7).

Hosted a Kids' Christmas Party with Love For Change Women's Foundation, granting wishes for 40 children.

Impact

In just a few years, we have witnessed remarkable improvements in our children's learning abilities. Most can now read, write and do basic arithmetics , thanks to our dedicated programs and partnerships.
